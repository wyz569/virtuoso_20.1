How do I visualize congestion in the Global Cell Track Utilization table?

After congestion analysis has been run, the Global Cell Track Utilization table is populated with the capacity and availability information of every gcell in the design. The Global Cell Track Utilization table is an effective and informative arrangement to quickly view the gcell information.
