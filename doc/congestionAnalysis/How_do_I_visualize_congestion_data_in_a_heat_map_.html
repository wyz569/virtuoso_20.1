How do I visualize congestion data in a heat map?

Use the heat map to visualize and analyze the illustrated congestion result of the design.

The heat map shows the levels of congestion in different colors. 

  • Cold colors, such as blue and green, indicate areas that are less congested.
  • Warmer colors, such as yellow, orange, and red, indicate areas that are highly congested.
  • Hot colors, such as purple and white, indicate areas of over-congestion.
