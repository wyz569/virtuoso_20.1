How can I sort the scenic ratio of nets?

You can sort the scenic ratio of nets in ascending or descending order.

  1. To sort the scenic ratio, click the Scenic Ratio column header.
    Clicking the column header the first time displays the scenic ratio of nets in ascending order. This means that the net with the lowest value is displayed at the top of the table.
  2. Click the column header again to display the scenic ratios in descending order.
