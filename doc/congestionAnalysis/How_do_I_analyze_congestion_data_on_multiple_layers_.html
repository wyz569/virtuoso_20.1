How do I analyze congestion data on multiple layers?

To analyze congestion data on multiple layers:

  1. Click the drop-down arrow next to the Filter Global Cells by icon on the Congestion Analysis toolbar.
  2. Click the Layer Display option. 
  3. Choose the appropriate layers for which you want to display the congestion data. You can choose: All Layers On, All Layers Off, Horizontal Layers Only, or Vertical Layers Only.
  4. Click OK.
