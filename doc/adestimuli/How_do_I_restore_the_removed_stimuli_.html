How do I restore the removed stimuli?

To restore the removed stimuli, follow the steps:

  1. Ensure that the authoring mode is disabled by selecting the Off option in the Stimuli Authoring section.
  2. Select one or more stimuli names. 
  3. Right-click and choose Restore Stimuli to Original Repository. It fetches all the information from stimuliRemovedNames.xml file.
  4. To see the restored stimuli, right-click and choose Display Available Stimuli.
