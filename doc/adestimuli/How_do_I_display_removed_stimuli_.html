Product Version ICADVM20.1, October 2020

How do I display removed stimuli?

To display the list of removed stimuli, right-click anywhere in the Stimuli Authoring section and choose Display Removed Stimuli.

Ensure that the authoring mode is disabled by selecting the Off option in the Stimuli Authoring section while performing the above action.

The Stimuli Authoring section is replaced by Removed Stimuli, which lists the removed stimuli in red. After reviewing the list of stimuli in this list, you can perform the following two actions:

  • Restore removed stimuli
  • Permanently delete stimuli
