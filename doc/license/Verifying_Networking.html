Verifying Networking

On the command prompt, enter the following:

ping localhost

 

You should be able to successfully ping yourself. If the ping fails, then you need to correct it. Without networking, FlexNet licensing will not work. For troubleshooting, see Q. What do I do to make a standalone machine work as a license server? (Applicable for Linux and Windows) .
