Auto Device Placement and Routing Quick Help
Product Version ICADVM20.1, October 2020

How can I control the direction in which devices are placed when they are moved?

Use the following interactive placement options in the context menu to control the direction of placement of devices that are moved:

  • Spread Any: Moves devices in any direction to avoid overlaps. 
  • Spread X: Moves devices in the X-direction to avoid overlaps. An info balloon that summarizes the following information is displayed:
    • Total wire length
    • Change in wire length
    • Row to which the device has snapped
    • Snap pattern to which the devices have snapped
  • Insert as Row: When multiple devices are selected and moved, their placement is restricted to the current row.
  • Insert as Column: When multiple devices are selected and moved, their placement is restricted to the current column.
