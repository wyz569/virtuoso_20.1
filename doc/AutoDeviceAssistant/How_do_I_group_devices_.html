Auto Device Placement and Routing Quick Help
Product Version ICADVM20.1, October 2020

How do I group devices?

Interactive placement options are available in a context menu, which is displayed when you select devices and device groups in the layout canvas. The context menu includes the Group and Ungroup options, which let you group and ungroup the selected devices.
The relative positions of the grouped devices are always maintained when you perform operations such as move.

Grouped devices can be in the same or different rows. Devices in a group are abutted horizontally.

You can use the context menu to specify whether grouped devices must be stacked horizontally or vertically when they are moved.
