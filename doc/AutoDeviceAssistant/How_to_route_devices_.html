Auto Device Placement and Routing Quick Help
Product Version ICADVM20.1, October 2020

How to route devices?

The final step in the automated device-level layout flow is device routing. You can use the Tree Router, which is the automatic device-level router to route your design.

To route a design,

  1. Click Route Devices in the Auto Device P&R assistant to open the Router tab.
  2. Click Enable Tree Router in Wire Assistant to open the Wire Assistant. The Auto Device Routing preset is selected by default. In this mode, the Tree router is used for routing. The Tree router automatically identifies and routes mesh, ring (tap cell rings), and tree structures in the design.
  3. Select All in the Route Net field to generate the routes.
