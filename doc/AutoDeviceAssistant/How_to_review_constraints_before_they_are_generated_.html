How to review constraints before they are generated?


To review and edit the constraints as per your requirements before generating them in the layout canvas:

  1. Open the Constraints tab of the Auto Device P&R assistants.
  2. Ensure that the required circuit finders are selected in the Circuit Finders section.
  3. In the View and Edit Constraints section, right-click the constraints to display a shortcut menu. Use the options in the shortcut menu to perform the following tasks:
    - Zoom in to magnify the selected constraints.
    - Select the constraints to be generated.
    - Unselect the constraints that you do not want to generate.
    - Delete Constraint from the layout cellview.
